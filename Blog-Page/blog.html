Impact of Technology

Technology

Technology is the application of technical and scientific knowledge to the practical aims of human life to solve problems and make life easier and comfortable. In this modern era, technology is evolving day by day. It has become a part of our modern life.The quick advancement and widespread use of technology has fundamentally changed how people work, live, communicate, and engage with one another. Athough technology is helping humans in many positive ways but, as we know everything has both merits and demerits. Just like that, technology also has both positive aspects and negative aspects.

Positive Impact of Technology

Technology is enhancing day by day with many more features and it has many positive impact in human life. It has permanently altered our everyday lives, the way we used to live, work, interact and communicate with others. It has transformed a number of fields like communication, education, healthcare and general society advancement.

The revolution in communication is one of the most significant positive effect of technology. People all around the world are now connected because of the development of smartphones, laptops, social media, email and so on. Because of technology now it is possible to do real-time communication through messages. Even if people are very far from eachother, they can share their sarrow and happiness with eachother.

Along with communication, the transformation in education is also one of the most significant impact of technology. Students can get various knowledge as plenty of materials are available online such as digital textbooks, educational apps, online courses and many more. They can do reaearch and gain knowledge based on their interest in any field. Most importantly, the emergence of e-learning platforms has removed traditional barriers to education by allowing people to learn at their own pace.

Technology has become a key factor in advancement, affecting everything from healthcare to the workplace and how people communicate and learn.

Negative Impact of Technology

Although we can not deny that technology has brought many positive changes, it is necssary to acknowledge that along with positive impact its rapid growth has also introduced some negative impact on human life.

As we all know, technology has brought too many social platforms through which humans can digitally communicate with eachother and due to this there is less in-person interaction between people. People might communicate virtually more often than communicating personally which may result in feelings of loneliness and decrease in the quality of interactions between people.

Also, the rapid growth of online platforms by technology has opened new path for cybercrime and cyberbullying. As a result, especially those who are young may significantly suffer from psychological health. Along with this, prolonged usage of digital platform for work or for entertainment may cause eye strain, sleep disruption and higher risk of mental health disorders including anxiety and depression.

There is no denying that technology has greatly enhanced many aspects of our life but it is important to acknowledge and deal with the negative impacts as well.

Impact of Technology on Daily Life and on Business/Printing Business

Technology has great impact in human life affecting a various aspects of daily Life such as communication, education, employment, healthcare, entertainment and so on. Along with this, it has a significant and wide-ranging impact on printing business changing various aspects of business. Technology introduced digital printing and revolutionized the printing business.

Digital printing process has many more advantage than the traditional one. Such as digital printing is cost-effective, high-quality printing, faster, eco-friendly and so on. The printing process has become more faster and more efficient since the introduction of digital printing. High quality toners and liquid inks are used by digital press to produce clear images and better designs. It consumes less energy, produces less waste which results in decrease of carbon footprint so, siginificantly it is more eco-friendly and sustainable.

Hence, technology has had an innovative impact on the printing sector resulting the better sustainability and efficiency of printers.

Conclusion

In conclusion, we can say that technoloy has dynamic impact in human life in every sector. It is constantly evolving human life and creating a foundation for future which will be even more inventive and creative. But, along with the advancement of technology it may bring some drawbacks so it is necessary to recognized and deal with it and also we need to be prepared for the upcoming one.
